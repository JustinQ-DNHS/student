## Grammar

<table>
    <tr>
        <td>Phrase</td>
        <td>一。。。就 (pg. 97 在课本)</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Meaning</td>
        <td>As soon as; Whenever; Once</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td rowspan="2">How to use?</td>
        <td>[Subject] 一 [Action A] 就 [Action B]</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>As soon as [Subject] does [Action A], they does [Action B]</td>
    </tr>
